The history of color: the power of color emotions

Author: Noguerón Méndez, José Antonio

Published 20-07-2022

The human being, throughout its history, has used different tools to discover, analyze and represent its environment. So the colors have served him as a symbolic instrument to achieve his goal. In this sense, this essay analyzes from a historical, psychological and scientific point of view the evolution of color and its consequences in various social and cultural areas.

Capture of pendular periods by means of photoresistors for the search of the terrestrial mass

Authors: Noguerón Méndez, José Antonio y Javier Moreno, Francisco Anthony

Published 22-07-2022

The mass of the earth has been of great importance throughout history, this being an important component for the greatest discoveries of humanity, in this article a theoretical and experimental model will be developed in order to obtain the terrestrial mass. For the theoretical model, the mechanics of the movement of a pendulum and the law of universal gravitation will be used, later an electronic measuring instrument was developed, in this way it would be possible to capture the pendulum movements for its theoretical application.

Applications of biomedical engineering for the diagnosis of diseases

Authors: Noguerón Méndez José Antonio, Javier Moreno Francisco Anthony, Louredes Farughi Salazar Medina, Victor Guillermo Miranda Martinez y Joselin Nicole Raymundo Rimachi

Published 24-02-2022

The main objective of this work is to present the applications of Biomedical Engineering for the diagnosis of diseases, based on two methods of recording biomedical signals, which are electromyography and electroneurography. Each of the methods and their usefulness in the diagnosis of neuromuscular diseases through the use of electrodes that store negative and positive charges are described. In addition, what are the difficulties in diagnosing these diseases, as well as the benefits of using the aforementioned methods and the tests that support it. From the above, it is concluded that biomedical engineering through the use of electromyography and electroneurography methods has allowed doctors to have a reliable tool to diagnose neuromuscular diseases.
