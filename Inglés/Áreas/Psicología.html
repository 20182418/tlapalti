The history of color: the power of color emotions

Author:Noguerón Méndez, José Antonio

Published 20-07-2022

The human being, throughout its history, has used different tools to discover, analyze and represent its environment. So the colors have served as a symbolic instrument to achieve his goal. In this sense, this essay analyzes from the historical, psychological and scientific point of view the evolution of color and its consequences in various social and cultural areas.

Learned helplessness: uncertainty in the psychic faculties

Authors: Noguerón Méndez, José Antonio y Javier Moreno, Francisco Anthony

Published 14-08-2022

In this research, the conception of learned helplessness and its derivatives in relation to the scientific area will be analyzed from the psychological and philosophical point of view. To reach the proposed objective, various points will be covered. First, to establish the frame of reference, a review of the study of emotion will be made, from the Aristotelian philosophy to the avant-garde currents, proceeding with the different periods in the study of it.
